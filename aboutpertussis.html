What is Pertussis?

Pertussis is a highly contagious bacterial disease that is very common in infants and children. It’s spread from from person to person through contact with fluids from an infected person’s mouth or nose. When infected, bacteria called Bordetella Pertussis attach to and line the upper respiratory system. Symptoms include a severe cough and fever. It’s also common to vomit due to coughing fits.
